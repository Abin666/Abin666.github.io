About Me

I am pursuing a bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, Coimbatore, India.

I am an Entrepreneur, a Full- Stack Web-developer, a Graphic designer, a UI and UX Developer by passion, a basketball lover and an artist. I believe that art is not what you simply see but it's what you feel deep within when you see something. And that is what I try to bring up in all the designs for the different platform that I make. Playing with colors is not an easy task, colors are a language that you speak. They act as a means of communication between an artist and the viewer. Bringing up the sense of perfection is what I intend to display in my designs. I have learns this by knowledge as well as experience.
